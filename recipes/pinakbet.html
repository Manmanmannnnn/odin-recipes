Pinakbet

Pinakbet is a traditional Filipino vegetable dish made with a mix of fresh vegetables, such as eggplant, squash, and bitter melon,
usually cooked with pork or shrimp. It’s known for its savory flavor, often enhanced with shrimp paste, making it a delicious and healthy option.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Bitter melon/li>
  • Eggplant
  • Squash
  • String beans
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Water
  • Pepper
  • Shrimp paste

Steps for Cooking

  1. Cook the Pork: In a pot, sauté the sliced onion and tomatoes until soft. Add the pork and cook until it turns brown.
  2. Add Water: Pour in enough water to cover the pork. Bring to a boil and simmer until the pork is tender.
  3. Add Vegetables: Once the pork is tender, add the squash, eggplant, string beans, and bitter melon to the pot. Stir well.
  4. Season: Add the shrimp paste (bagoong) and mix everything together. Let it cook for about 5-10 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve: Check the seasoning and adjust if needed. Serve hot with rice and enjoy your pinakbet!
